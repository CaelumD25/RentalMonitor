You asked, we listened. Swipe/Click through to learn about important improvements taking place Used.ca.
Easier search
The layout and functionality of the search bar has been improved. You can now search ”all categories” or drill down to a specific category to find what you’re looking for faster.
My alerts
Preview of ads you have alerts set for will now appear in the "My alerts" section in your profile.
Multiple regions
This is BIG! Previously, you had to navigate to an entirely new Used.ca city site to search that region. Now, you can search multiple regions from any city site. Helpful when looking for a hard-to-find item or specialty service. You can search by location name or by radius.

Now you can buy a cost-effective ad upgrade so that your ad will be seen by users not searching in your specific area. For example, if you’re located in Victoria, but you want users in Vancouver to see your ad, this upgrade is for you!
